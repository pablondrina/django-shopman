Hosted by ``storefront/cart.html`` and returned standalone by ``CartPageContentView`` so the page swaps in place without a full reload after every stepper, coupon, or upsell action. Consumes ``CartProjection`` (typed read model). Stepper, delete and upsell use Alpine + native ``fetch`` then dispatch ``cartUpdated`` — ``htmx.ajax`` is avoided here because the cascading reload of this wrapper destroys the source element mid-swap-pipeline, which trips htmx's post-swap cleanup with a TypeError.
